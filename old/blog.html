YOLO - Some meaningful insight...

"I wish my stove came with a Save As button like Word has. That way I could experiment with my cooking and not fear ruining my dinner." - Jarod Kintz

I believe in redefining the way people do everyday tasks. Forget creating crazy, wacky new technology that will extend our horizons. No, I want to solve all the smaller problems we face everyday but are too lazy to tackle. That way, life becomes something exciting to wake up to. We live one day without knowledge of what will happen the next - so why waste today dreaming about tomorrow? To all of you I say #YOLO. Let's change Today.


Failure

"There's a way to do it better - find it." - Thomas Edison

I create innovative solutions to everyday problems in creative, novel ways. Innovation is having the courage to sacrifice the comfortable, reliable norm to start all over again. Failure isn't a possibility; it is an expectation. I embrace failure as a breadcrumb on the trail to success. And so we have the startling reality of innovation: Today's success is tomorrow's failure. So it goes.
